Origin Story

Kyoto, Japa

In Kyoto, Japan, "H-Thai-ML" is a Thai restaurant with a coding twist. Founded by Hiroshi Tanaka, a software engineer with a love for Thai cuisine, the restaurant seamlessly blends Thai flavors with technology. Its unique features include AI-powered ordering through "TasteBot" and a fusion of traditional Thai aesthetics with high-tech design. H-Thai-ML has become a favorite among tech enthusiasts and foodies, showcasing how a passion for coding and a love for food can create a unique dining experience in the heart of Kyoto
